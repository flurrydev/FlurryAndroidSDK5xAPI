Detailed Description

Targeting settings for ads.

Author
2009-2014 Flurry, Inc. All Rights Reserved

Targeting allows partners to supply information they track internally, which is not available to Flurry's targeting system. If the targeting settings are matched on the ad server, a subset of targeted ads will be delivered to the user.
