void setTargeting ( FlurryAdTargeting  targeting)

Set the ad targeting.

Set the targeting for this ad. Targeting allows partners to supply information they track internally, which is not available to Flurry's targeting system. If the targeting settings are matched on the ad server, a subset of targeted ads will be delivered to the user.
